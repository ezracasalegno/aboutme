Mars: The Future of Humanity

  1. Atmosphere and Temperature:

    The Martian atmosphere is very thin, less than 1% as dense as Earth's.

    • It is composed of over 95% carbon dioxide.
    • The average temperature is about -63.
    • Mars has a pinkish-red sky due to the fine dust particles suspended in the air.
  2. Landmarks and Size:

    Mars is home to the largest volcano in the solar system and a massive canyon system.

    • Olympus Mons is a shield volcano three times taller than Mount Everest.
    • Valles Marineris is a canyon system that runs for over 4,000 km (2,500 miles).
    • Mars is only about half the diameter of Earth.
  3. Moons and Orbit:

    Mars has two small, irregularly shaped moons.

    • Phobos is the larger and orbits very close to the planet.
    • Deimos is smaller and orbits further away.
    • A Martian day (sol) is 24.6 hours, very similar to Earth's!
